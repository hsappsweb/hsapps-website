Streamline population management efforts by optimizing workflow throughout the catch, neuter & release process. Field users gather field & clinical data for program monitoring using a mobile app while administrators setup projects and analyze results through a web app.

Features

Data Gathering with Offline/Sync Support

In addition to recording basic animal data (breed, gender, etc..) the app will automatically capture the geolocation of the animal’s ‘home’ and capture a photo.

The app is designed for spotty or poorly performing internet connectivity and will cache and sync all data when connectivity is restored.

Proper data collection means tamper-proof data and enhanced welfare standards

Release Map & GPS “lock”
Optimally plan your release day

GPS Lock: At release time, the app will require release with a 15M radius of the animal’s ‘home’ or found location.

Surgery Data Gathering
Record surgery data quickly and easily with the phone app and scan RF-ID tag barcodes

Provide accuracy and easy management system

Project Setup & Analysis
Dynamic reporting system with options to view and download in different formats.
