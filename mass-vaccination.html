Optimize mass vaccination efforts by leveraging dedicated field (mobile) and administrative (web) apps. Large projects are divided up into smaller areas (geofences) for vaccination and survey work.  The field app increases team throughput and the web app ensures program managers meet their vaccination objectives.

Features

Geofencing

Divide and conquer large vaccination efforts by splitting teams into smaller areas. Setting up a geo-fence (virtual boundary) for each team will ensure no overlap in vaccination efforts. The app will keep users in their assigned area and warn them if they go outside the geo-fence. 

Real-time Dashboards & Data review

View progress in real-time, on maps.

Users can check the areas remained uncovered or any small street they may have left out.

Enhance productivity and increase coordination.

Project Setup & Analysis
Web application provides dynamic reporting system with options to view and download in different formats.

 
 

Survey Feature

Verify areas reach vaccination target levels
